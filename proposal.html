CrpytoBase - A social platform for cryptocurrency investments

  • RESTFul API's: Mediastack for crypto news, Coingecko for cryptocurrency data

  • The data provided by Mediastack will give the users news of their favorite cryptocurrency and Coingecko will provide information about the cryptocurrencies like - price, market-cap, volume etc.

  • Data collected from authenticated users:

    Authenticated users will choose their prospective cryptocurrencies and be able to like news/blogs which will be stored in our SQLite databse.

  • User Data Table:
    firstName, lastName, password(encrypted), dob, email, userID
    Favourite Cryptos Table:
    cryptoID, cryptoTicker, cryptoName
    News Items:
    newsID, userID, NewsURL, cryptoTicker, numLikes
    Comments Table:
    commentID, userID, newsID, comment
  • Target Users:

    This website will be useful for crypto traders to get the trending news about cryptocurrencies and make informed decisions before putting their money in the market.

  • Goals:
    1. To give users personalised news about their favorite cryptocurrency.
    2. To give users the ability to perform fundamental analysis on the cryptocurrency to get better understanding.
  • Why we selected this topic:

    We selected this topic to create awareness about cryptocurrency as people loose a lot of money because of the high volatility in the crypto market as well as because of our interest in learning more about it.
